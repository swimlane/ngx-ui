Split

Resizable split layout. Use swim-split with swim-split-area and swim-split-handle as direct children. Drag the handle to resize; double-click the handle to snap to min or max.

Horizontal split with two areas. Left fills remaining space; right starts at 50% and cannot grow.

Left panel

Drag the handle to resize. Double-click the handle to snap to extremes.

Right panel (50%, cannot grow)

Import the split components (and icon for the handle grip). Use area-basis, min-basis, max-basis on areas; handle-basis on the handle (default 15px). 

<script type="module">
  import '@swimlane/lit-ui/icon';
  import '@swimlane/lit-ui/split';
</script>

<div class="split-example">
  <swim-split direction="row">
    <swim-split-area area-basis="1 1 50%">Left content</swim-split-area>
    <swim-split-handle></swim-split-handle>
    <swim-split-area area-basis="1 1 50%">Right content</swim-split-area>
  </swim-split>
</div>
